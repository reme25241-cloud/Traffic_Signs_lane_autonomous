üß† Traffic Sign Classification (AI Layer)

The system uses a Teachable Machine image classification model trained on 20 different traffic sign classes, including:

  • Stop Sign
  • No Entry
  • Zebra Crossing
  • Maximum Speed
  • Traffic Signal
  • School Ahead
  • No Parking
  • Train Crossing
  • One Way
  • U-Turn
  • Left Turn
  • Right Turn
  • No Overtaking
  • Two Way Road
  • Narrow Road
  • Bumps
  • Horn Prohibited
  • Restricted Zone
  • Aid Station
  • Diesel Fuel

Predictions are accepted only when the confidence score is 90% or higher, ensuring reliable classification.

üóÑÔ∏è Database Design

The database is structured for scalability and analytics:

  • SignUpload: Stores original uploaded images
  • SignPrediction: Stores predicted sign, confidence, processed images, and timestamps

This design allows multiple predictions per image and clean historical analysis.
